About

NSS IIT Ropar is a voluntary group of students and faculties struggling and fighting stringently for the betterment of society . The National Service Scheme (NSS) is an Indian government-sponsored public service program conducted by the Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports of the Government of India.

The motto of our NSS is “ Not Me But You”. As this motto itself reflects the service and selflessness to bring a happy change in Indian society. NSS , also is taken up by students as a course for 4 semesters under CRP. They are the social workers of the institute and are divided into well-coordinated groups of about 100 students under a faculty incharge. It’s goal is not only to serve people but also to inculcate the spirit of social welfare, communal health and thoughts of education among the people.

Under this organization, Human value talks, blood donation camp, tree plantation, helping NGO – Pehchaan Ek Safar in spreading education and many other social related tasks are performed. We spread awareness required to diminish bad habits like smoking , consuming alcohol etc. By organising camps. Recently, a program named SARP- MITRA was organized by us to survive in snake free campus.
