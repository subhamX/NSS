Project Meetings

Aug 26, 2019

Meeting to discuss various project ideas. And decide on our final project. We had many ideas like organizing a talk, calling an alumnus but after mutual discussion, we realized it is better to create a website for NSS where all the details can be put up.

Aug 09, 2019

We discussed our idea with the instructor to get his approval. He also gave his valuable input by explaining what he expects from the website and what purpose it is supposed to serve.

Sep 14, 2019

We reported our progress and plans for our instructor.

Sep 16, 2019

We discussed various prospect templates for our website. We finally decided to use a free template, but we were still not sure about exactly which template should be used.

Sep 21, 2019

The instructor showed us the website of NSS IIT Delhi to explain to us about templates that can be used and also about the simplicity we want on our website.

Sep 23, 2019

We met to decide which stats and details are required to create our website. We also decided on what content is to be put up on the website and from which sources it can be collected.

Sep 30, 2019

We met to see and analyze various other websites so that we can decide upon the design part of our own website and come on consensus about the design part of our website.

Oct 4, 2019

We finally met to divide work amongst ourselves so that everyone has equal contribution in the project, we also asked them to do a little research on their job so that in the next session we can confirm our roles.

Oct 11, 2019

We finally confirmed our roles in the project and also discussed the final detailed road map to create our website..
